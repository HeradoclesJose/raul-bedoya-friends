Who I am?

"I am a VISUAL COMMUNICATOR that connects God with people through my creations, transmitting the language of Love, Enthusiasm, Faith and Success."

Hello My name is Raul Bedoya Chingote, Venezuelan, dedicated for more than 20 years to graphic design, graphic and print arts specializing in the area of ​​packaging and illustration.
For more than 7 years I dedicated myself to work Illustrating and designing for the children and youth area, making drawings or illustration for toys, album design of cards, figurines and Trading cards, magazines, stories and comics, creating characters, making board games. among many other printed products.
After many years I created my company to manufacture packaging for stores and gifts making it my business plan, called Origami c.a. and with web in www.fabricadeempaques.com and in free moments I dedicate my time to improve and discover new tools that improve my professional capacity, that is why I have learned to work with Animation or graphics in motion for videos and Development of Web Pages like this in which you are now for example. Today I continue designing new projects until I see them materialized as I love entrepreneurship in the graphic field and I take advantage of this to carry out social development by offering and teaching the development work of handmade products based on paper, cardboard and fabric. this way I contribute my small grain of sand to the social area and at the same time having a philosophy of conservation to the environment.
